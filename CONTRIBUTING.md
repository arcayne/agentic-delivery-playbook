# Contributing

Contributions are welcome.

Good contributions usually improve one of these areas:

- clearer workflow language
- better templates
- more realistic examples
- adapters for other coding-agent harnesses
- failure-mode and QA guidance

Please keep the playbook portable. Provider-specific or harness-specific instructions should live under `adapters/`.

## Principles

- Do not make small direct edits unnecessarily ceremonial.
- Keep human approval gates explicit.
- Prefer evidence over agent summaries.
- Record model/agent routing honestly.
- Treat repeated fix cycles as a signal, not as normal progress.
